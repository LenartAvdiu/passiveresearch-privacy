Delete Your Account

Last updated: May 2026

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open the PassiveResearch app.
  2. Tap the Account tab.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  4. Type DELETE in the confirmation field and confirm.

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted immediately.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and authentication credentials
  • All saved items and collections
  • All generated outputs and schedules
  • Any preferences or settings stored on our servers

What is retained

  • Anonymised, aggregated analytics that cannot be linked back to you
  • Transaction records required by law (e.g. purchase receipts) for up to 7 years

Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email contact@rockskippers.com with the subject "Delete my account". We will process your request within 30 days.
