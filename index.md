# Privacy Policy — Passive Research

**Last updated: May 1, 2026**

This policy applies to all Passive Research products: the iOS app, Android app, and Chrome extension.

---

## What we collect

**Account information**
Your name and email address, collected when you sign in with Apple or Google.

**Saved content**
URLs and metadata (titles, thumbnails, captions) for items you save through the app or Chrome extension.

**Generated outputs**
Research summaries, newsletters, and articles generated inside the app.

**Account identifier**
A unique ID linked to your account, used to sync your saves and outputs across your devices and platforms (iOS, Android, Chrome extension).

**Push notification token**
If you enable notifications, a device token is sent to our servers to deliver them. It is not used for any other purpose.

**API keys (BYOK)**
If you choose to add your own Anthropic or OpenAI API key, it is stored securely on your device. When you generate content, your key is transmitted to our servers solely to process that request. It is not stored on our servers and is never used for any other purpose.

---

## How we store it

Your data is stored securely using Supabase. We do not sell your data to third parties.

---

## Third-party services

**Apple Sign-In / Google Sign-In** — used for authentication. Governed by Apple's and Google's privacy policies respectively.

**RevenueCat** — used to manage subscriptions. Governed by RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

**Anthropic / OpenAI** — only contacted if you provide your own API key and trigger a generation. Governed by their respective privacy policies.

---

## Deleting your account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly inside the app: go to the Account tab and tap Delete Account. This action is immediate and cannot be undone.
