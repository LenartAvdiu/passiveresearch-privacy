# Privacy Policy — Passive Research

**Last updated: April 27, 2026**

## What we collect

- **Account information** — your name and email address, collected when you sign in with Google.
- **Saved content** — URLs and metadata (titles, thumbnails, captions) for items you save through the app or Chrome extension.
- **Generated outputs** — research summaries and articles generated inside the app.
- **API keys** — if you choose to add your own Anthropic or OpenAI key, it is stored locally on your device only and never sent to our servers.

## How we store it

Your data is stored securely using Supabase. We do not sell your data to third parties.

## Third-party services

- **Google Sign-In** — used for authentication. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
- **RevenueCat** — used to manage subscriptions. Governed by RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
- **Anthropic / OpenAI** — only used if you provide your own API key. Requests are sent directly from your device.

## Deleting your account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly inside the app: go to the **Account tab** and tap **Delete Account**. This action is immediate and cannot be undone.
